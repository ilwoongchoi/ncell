Employee Greeting

In the future, the planet is expected to face environmental crises and food crises due to climate change and reduced populations of animals and plants caused by greenhouse gases, putting our lives at risk as a whole.

 

N-CELL has the research power and technology to contribute to the future through the bio-industry by large-scale cultivation, extraction and production of functional materials using technology optimized through microorganisms for producing edible and pharmaceutical raw materials.


We intend to grow into a company committed to the health and well-being of mankind by continuously promoting the development of technology necessary for the development of functional raw materials based on microbial and animal cells.
